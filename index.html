About

Infrastructure strategist, entrepreneur, operator.

Charging infrastructure should feel as thoughtful as the vehicles it supports.

My work bridges the gap between technical infrastructure and hospitality, creating charging solutions that prioritize both performance and experience. I specialize in developing premium charging destinations along underserved corridors, integrating renewable energy systems, and building operational frameworks that deliver exceptional reliability in challenging locations.

With 15+ years in IT operations across healthtech, fintech, and hospitality, at properties like Wynn Las Vegas, The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas, Grand Hyatt Nashville, and Sensei wellness retreats, I bring a systems-thinking approach to every challenge. I've architected everything from enterprise identity management to compliance frameworks, always favoring solutions that are practical, scalable, and built to last.

Through leadership in EV owner communities, I keep a direct line to driver needs and charging pain points. My goal is simple: transform EV charging from a logistics burden into an integral, enjoyable part of the journey.
